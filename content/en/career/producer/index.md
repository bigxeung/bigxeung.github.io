## 💡 About Me

### Strengths
- **Hip-Hop Expertise**: Deep understanding from the latest trends like Rage and Trap to the roots of Boom Bap.
- **Genre Versatility**: Ability to produce beats that fit global trends beyond hip-hop, including R&B, Afro, and Reggaeton.
- **Unique Sound**: Skilled in building my own signature sound through the Alternative genre.

### Vision
- **Signature Sound**: My goal is to establish my signature sound by collaborating with top domestic and international artists.
- **Trendsetter**: I aim to lead the market with new sounds, not just follow trends.
- **Global Collaboration**: I seek to broaden my musical spectrum through active collaboration with artists beyond Korea.

### Workflow
- **Responsive to Feedback**: I prioritize the artist's vision and aim for fast, precise communication.
- **Efficient Speed**: My systematic process allows for the rapid delivery of high-quality results.
- **Collaborative Synergy**: I believe in creating results greater than the sum of their parts through synergy with the artist.
